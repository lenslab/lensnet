Language and Listening Study for Adults

We are conducting a study on language and listening in adults that are 18-40 years old and only use spoken English. You may be eligible to participate if you: (1) have no hearing loss or difficulty listening OR (2) have used a hearing aid or cochlear implant since 5 years old.

We are studying how adults process speech. During this study, you will listen to and make decisions about sentences. We will use neuroimaging that uses harmless light to measure brain activity and thermal imaging to measure the temperature of your face. This study will take approximately 1.5 hours, and you will be compensated $20 per hour for your participation.
