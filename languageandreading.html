Language and Reading Study for Children

This study uses functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS) neuroimaging, a non-invasive technology that uses harmless light to track brain activity, while children perform simple language and reading tasks on a computer. If your child is deaf and knows ASL or is hearing and only knows English, your child may be eligible to participate! The study takes about an hour and you will be compensated $20/hour for your child's time and your travel to and from the lab.
